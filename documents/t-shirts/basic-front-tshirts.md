---

## Design Format

**Structure:** Simple centered design on front
- Quote/text (main element)
- Small icon/image (optional accent)
- Clean, readable fonts
- High contrast colors

## üé® Design Guidelines

### Font Recommendations
- **Bold & Readable:** Bebas Neue, Impact, Arial Black
- **Fun & Friendly:** Komika, Comic Sans alternatives
- **Professional:** Helvetica Bold, Futura Bold
- **Handwritten:** Permanent Marker, Indie Flower

### Color Schemes
**Classic:**
- Black on white
- White on black
- Navy on white

**Healthcare:**
- NHS Blue (#005EB8) on white
- Medical Red (#DC143C) on white
- Scrubs Green (#008080) on white

**Trendy:**
- Coral (#FF6B6B) on navy
- Teal (#20B2AA) on charcoal
- Mustard (#FFD700) on black

### Sizing Standards
- Small design: 6-8 inches wide
- Medium design: 8-10 inches wide
- Large design: 10-12 inches wide
- Position: 3-4 inches below collar

---

## üí° Design Tips

1. **Keep it simple** - One message, one small icon max
2. **High contrast** - Text must be easily readable from distance
3. **Test readability** - View design from 10 feet away
4. **Consider shirt colors** - Design must work on multiple colors
5. **Avoid fine details** - Simple, bold designs print better
6. **Think about audience** - Would an HCP wear this to work? (under scrubs) or socially?

## üè≠ Production Notes (Gelato)

**Recommended T-Shirt Specs:**
- 100% cotton or cotton blend
- Unisex/relaxed fit most popular
- Colors: White, Black, Navy, Heather Grey
- Sizes: XS-3XL
- Print area: Max 12" x 16" (front)

**File Requirements:**
- PNG with transparent background
- 300 DPI minimum
- RGB color mode
- Large enough for maximum print size
