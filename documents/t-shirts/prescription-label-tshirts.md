## ğŸ¨ Design Specifications

### Visual Elements
- **Background:** White or cream (prescription pad color)
- **Border:** Simple medical form border
- **Font:** Typewriter or medical form font (Courier, Monaco)
- **NHS Logo:** Small logo at top (optional, check trademark)
- **Rx Symbol:** Medical prescription symbol
- **Barcode:** Optional fake barcode at bottom for authenticity

### Layout Options

**Option A: Badge Style (Small)**
- 6"x8" size
- Positioned center chest (like hospital ID badge)
- Compact, subtle design
- Great for wearing under scrubs

**Option B: Full Front (Large)**
- 10"x12" size
- Center front placement
- Full prescription visible
- Statement piece

**Option C: Pocket Style**
- 4"x5" size
- Upper left chest (pocket area)
- Minimalist
- Professional look
