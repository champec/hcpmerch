## üé® Design Specifications

### Standard Sizes (Gelato)
- **11oz mug:** Most common, everyday size
- **15oz mug:** Large size for serious coffee needs
- Both available in:
  - White ceramic (standard)
  - Black ceramic (for white/light designs)
  - Colored mugs (limited options)

### Print Areas
- **Full wrap:** 360¬∞ design around mug
- **Standard:** Front design (8.5" x 3.5")
- **Two-sided:** Front and back designs

### Design Guidelines
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI minimum
- **Safe area:** Keep important elements 0.5" from edges
- **Handle side:** Consider right and left-handed users
- **Microwave safe:** Use dishwasher/microwave safe prints
